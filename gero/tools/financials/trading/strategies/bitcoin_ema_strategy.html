üí° Pro Trading Tips for OKX

1. Confirmation is Key

Never enter immediately on the crossover. Wait for the 1-hour candle to close completely to confirm the signal. False crossovers are common in choppy markets.

2. Use Limit Orders

On OKX spot, use limit orders instead of market orders to avoid slippage and get better fills. Place your limit slightly above current price for buys, below for sells.

3. Multiple Timeframe Analysis

Check the 4H and daily charts before entering. Only take long signals when higher timeframes show uptrend. This significantly improves win rate.

4. Avoid Range-Bound Markets

EMA crossovers work best in trending markets. If Bitcoin is consolidating in a tight range, wait for a breakout before trading.

5. Partial Profit Taking

Take 50% profits at 1:2 risk-reward, let remaining 50% run to 1:3 or trail stop. This locks in gains while capturing bigger moves.

6. Maximum Daily Risk

Never risk more than 6% of your account in a single day. After 3 losing trades in a row, stop trading for the day and review your strategy.

8. News & Events

Avoid trading 30 minutes before/after major economic news (FOMC, CPI, jobs data). Bitcoin can gap violently through your stops during these events.

üéØ Advanced Techniques

EMA Bounce Strategy

In a strong trend, don't wait for crossovers. Instead, buy when price pulls back to the 21 EMA and bounces. This catches entries earlier in the trend.

Volume Confirmation

Strong crossovers are accompanied by 50%+ increase in volume. If volume is weak on crossover, reduce position size or skip the trade.

RSI Filter

Add RSI indicator (14 period). Only take long signals when RSI > 50, short signals when RSI < 50. This filters out counter-trend trades.

Trailing Stop

Once in profit by 1:1 R:R, move stop to breakeven. At 1:2, trail stop below the 21 EMA to lock in profits while letting winners run.
