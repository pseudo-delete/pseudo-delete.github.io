Gero's Fight Arena
Potential new title: Cycle 1 Gero's Fight Arena or Cycle 1 is a card-fight game where local residents climb up their ladder on the way on being in the highest form of life heirarchy, coming from a local resident, with each and every training of each cycle, will lead closer into being the strongest form of life with a godly strength. The fight cycle never ends.
